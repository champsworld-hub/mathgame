❌ What if I get it wrong?
0

Zero points. Every time.

If your answer is wrong, you get 0 — no matter how fast you were or how hard the level.

Look at your history to see the right answer, learn from it, and try again. Every mistake is a chance to get better! 🧠

🔥 Keep your streak alive!

Every correct answer in a row grows your streak. Miss one and it resets to zero. Your best streak is saved forever!

💡 Tips to get a high score
🧠
Keep a running total in your headAfter each number appears, add or subtract it immediately. Don't wait until the end!
👀
Use "Peek" wiselyThe peek button shows previous numbers — but using it takes time. Save it for when you're really stuck!
🎯
Start easy, build upMaster Easy mode first. When you can get it right quickly every time, move to Medium. Your multiplier will skyrocket!
⚙️
Custom mode = HUGE scores6-digit numbers with many terms? The multiplier is enormous — but it's very hard. Only try it when you're ready!
